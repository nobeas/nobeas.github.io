Dr. Isaac Beas is an experienced research scientist and academic professional with over 10 years of proven expertise spanning physical chemistry, heterogeneous catalysis, materials science, and sustainable energy applications including gasification and pyrolysis of biomass. His research portfolio bridges fundamental scientific inquiry with practical industrial applications, focusing on waste valorization and circular economy approaches. Beyond his core research in materials science, Isaac has developed significant expertise in data science and computational methods, applying machine learning and natural language processing techniques to advance materials research and drive data-informed decision-making in chemical engineering applications. As an educator and mentor, Isaac has demonstrated excellence in teaching at university level and supervising postgraduate students. He maintains active community partnerships and industry collaborations, translating research outcomes into real-world impact. His work has resulted in a strong publication record with contributions to peer-reviewed journals across materials science, catalysis, and sustainable technologies. Isaac remains committed to advancing chemical education and research excellence in African institutions, working to build capacity and foster innovation that addresses both local and global challenges in sustainable materials and energy systems.
